Transportation

From Incheon Airport to Incheon Songdo

ICN is an airport near Songdo, Incheon.
There are two ways to get to Incheon National University Station in Songdo, Incheon.

Taxi

The Incheon National airport is ranked one of the top airports in the world and is just 20 minutes by car or bus from INU's main campus in Songdo and these are your best transportation options as there is no direct subway route to Songdo. Taxi is another option (about KRW30,000). There will be a toll cost of about KRW to cross the Incheon bridge if you rent a car.

Bus

Terminal 2-B1 (Bus stop number 35612)
Terminal 1 1F-2B (35202), 13B (35205)

Bus number: 6777

Final destination First bus T1 07:30 / T2 07:10 Last bus T1 22:00 / T2 21:40
Fare ₩7,000

Subway

It takes about 110 minutes from Incheon Airport to Central Park Hotel.

Incheon Airport Terminal 2 (or Terminal 1) - Keyang - (transfer to Subway Incheon line 1) - Incheon Start Up Park (Incheon National University Station)
