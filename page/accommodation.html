Hotel

The Central Park Hotel Songdo

Website: https://songdocentralparkhotel.southkrhotel.com/en/

We have blocked rooms at The Central Park Hotel Songdo and encourage you to take advantage of the special rate by staying at the designated hotel. Please, note that there is limited availability.

Rate: The special rate for a double or twin bed is approximately 136,000 Korean Won per night, including tax and complimentary breakfast.

Reservation: To reserve the room, please fill out the form and send it via email to the Hotel directly at rsvn@cphotel.co.kr.

Access from Incheon Airport to The Central Park Hotel Songdo: Maps

Train:
It takes about 110 minutes from Incheon Airport to Central Park Hotel.
Incheon Airport Terminal 2 (or Terminal 1) - Keyang - (transfer to Subway Incheon line 1) - Central Park Station (5 minutes walk) - Central Park Hotel Songdo.

Taxi:
It takes about 35 minutes from Incheon Airport to Central Park Hotel.
The fare is about 25,000 Korean Won (Terminal 1) and about 26,000 Korean Won (Terminal 2).
