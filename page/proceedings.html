About

All participants (not only speakers) can submit their manuscripts of a research paper of high quality to the proceedings,
where the contents does not need to be the same as their lectures given in the conference. The proceedings will be issued by World Scientific Publishing.
They will send proceedings to each university of participants.

Editors of manuscripts

To be announced

How to submit

Participants should submit their tex file and pdf file to corresnponding editor by e-mail.

Pages

In total 300 pages.
The maximum page length is as follows.

>> 30 pages for each of the eight invited speakers (preferably about 20 pages).
>> 15 pages for each of other participants (preferably about 10 pages).

Dead lines

Submission: To be announced
Referee's reports: To be announced
