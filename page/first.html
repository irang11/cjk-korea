The Ninth China-Japan-Korea International Conference on Ring and Module Theory

Aim of the Conference

The aim of the conference is to facilitate the exchange of current research ideas and to contribute to mutual communications and possible collaborations for ring and module theory in China-Japan-Korea. Ring and module theorists of other countries are also very much welcomed to the conference.

Period and Venue

August 14 (Mon) - 19 (Sat), 2023

Incheon National University
119, Academy-ro, Yeonsu-gu, Incheon, South Korea
